# Design Directions — Themes

Five themes. Same layout, different feeling. Switcher in Settings persists choice.

---

### Warm Paper (`warm_paper`) — default
- **Feeling:** an afternoon note on a desk; warm, quiet, journal-like.
- **Colors:** cream bg `#F8F4EC`, paper surface, muted sage accent `#7D9A7A`, warm brown text.
- **Strength:** safest default, gentle, reads as "a friend's note".
- **Weakness:** can feel flat; needs a touch of texture/serif to fully sing.
- **Improve:** consider a serif for the trace sentence; subtle paper grain.

### Dusk Garden (`dusk_garden`)
- **Feeling:** the 20 minutes before sunset; calm, settling.
- **Colors:** blue-grey bg, pale lavender surface, soft amber accent `#D7A35F`.
- **Strength:** emotionally distinct, "wind down" tone.
- **Weakness:** amber accent can clash on green-ish content tags.
- **Improve:** tune accent-soft for tag legibility.

### Minimal iOS (`minimal_ios`)
- **Feeling:** native, clean, lots of whitespace, no task pressure.
- **Colors:** near-white, white cards, subtle blue accent.
- **Strength:** familiar, unintimidating; great for the iOS target.
- **Weakness:** risks feeling generic / less "soul".
- **Improve:** lean on type scale + spacing, not color, for warmth.

### Field Notebook (`field_notebook`)
- **Feeling:** sitting at the edge of a field writing one line.
- **Colors:** pale field green, notebook-paper surface, olive accent.
- **Strength:** matches exploration/outdoor seeds beautifully.
- **Weakness:** narrow emotional range (very "outdoors").
- **Improve:** a faint ruled-line motif on cards (optional).

### Soft Ritual (`soft_ritual`) — dark / late night
- **Feeling:** bedtime ritual; warm water, a lamp, not rescuing your life.
- **Colors:** warm dark brown-black, candle amber accent, cream text.
- **Strength:** perfect for late-night/stop-loss mode; protects the eyes.
- **Weakness:** only dark theme — should likely auto-suggest at late_night.
- **Improve:** auto-offer this theme when `isLateNight` (not yet wired).
